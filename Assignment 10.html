Using Special Characters

  • The ampersand (&) is written as &.
  • The less than symbol (<) is written as <.
  • The greater than symbol (>) is written as >.
  • The copyright symbol (©) is written as ©.
  • The registered trademark symbol (®) is written as ®.
  • The trademark symbol (™) is written as ™.
