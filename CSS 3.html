Serif Font style

A font style refers to the specific design and appearance of characters in a typeface. It encompasses various attributes, such as the shape, weight, spacing, and decorative elements of the letters. Font styles play a crucial role in visual communication by conveying a particular mood or conveying a message effectively. They can range from elegant and sophisticated to bold and attention-grabbing, each with its own unique characteristics. Choosing the right font style is essential in creating a cohesive and visually appealing design, whether it's for print or digital media.
